About us section:

Hi, I am Prashan Maharjan. I am a Bachelor's graduate with a degree in management. I find finance and economics really interesting because I can observe and feel the impacts of them in real life, and the causes and effects of these issues can be quantified. Not only that, but the numbers do have significance in the life of ordinary people. It is not some vague, abstract scientific stuff that one has a hard time wrapping their brains around. Based on those numbers, a policymaker can then make a sound decision.

In this blog site, I intend to provide information about the global economy in layman's terms, thus making it possible for ordinary people to understand the financial system.
